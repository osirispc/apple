##This document will show you how to add icons to the doc on the Mac Mini.  

**First copy over the icons to the Documents folder on the Mac Mini using the Remote Destop Tool from a Mac.  
**Then run the following commands in unix using the same tool.  

**Add icon to doc**
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/KOHO-Password-Manager.url

/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/Office.rdp
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/CareTracker.webloc
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/CBIZ.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/CDS.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/EMAR.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/HelpDesk.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/KOHO-Password-Manager.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/MyMitc.webloc
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/PIN-Login.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/Supervisor-Login.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/SharePoint.webloc

**New Links**
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/PIN-Login.webloc
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --add /Users/houseuser/Documents/Shortcuts/Supervisor-Login.webloc

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Move icon in doc**

/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move CareTracker.webloc --position 1 
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move CBIZ.url --position 2
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move EMAR.url --position 3
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move Relias.webloc --position 4
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move Office.rdp --position 5
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move HelpDesk.url --position 6
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move KOHO-Password-Manager.url --position 7
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move PIN-Login.url --position 8
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move Supervisor-Login.url --position 9
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move SharePoint.webloc --position 10
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move MyMitc.webloc --position 11


**New Position**
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move PIN-Login.webloc --position 8
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --move Supervisor-Login.webloc --position 9


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Remove icon from doc**

/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove Office.rdp
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove CareTracker.webloc
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove CBIZ.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove CDS.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove EMAR.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove HelpDesk.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove KOHO-Password-Recovery.url
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove MyMitc.webloc
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove 'Service Log PIN Login.url'
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove 'Service Log Supervisor Login.url'
/usr/local/bin/dockutil --remove SharePoint.webloc
